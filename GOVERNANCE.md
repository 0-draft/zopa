# Governance

## Roles

**Contributors** open issues and pull requests. Anyone is welcome.

**Maintainers** have commit access. The current list lives in
[MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

## Decisions

Day-to-day work runs on lazy consensus: a pull request that has at
least one maintainer approval and no maintainer objection after 72
hours can merge. Maintainers may merge their own PRs after a separate
maintainer's approval.

Changes that affect the project's direction (license, governance,
public ABI breaks, dependencies, repository ownership) require
explicit approval from a majority of maintainers.

Disagreements that can't be resolved through discussion fall back to
a simple majority vote among maintainers. Ties favor the status quo.

## Adding maintainers

Anyone with a track record of contribution and review may be
nominated. Approval follows the rules above for direction-level
decisions.

## Communication

Public discussion happens on GitHub Issues and Pull Requests. Private
maintainer-only matters (security disclosures, conduct reports) are
handled by direct contact with the people listed in
[MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

## Code of conduct

All participation is governed by [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
